## FAQ

**Can Chinese mainland users register?**
Yes. Both Binance and OKX accept Chinese national ID for KYC verification as of March 2026.

**Is it legal to buy Bitcoin in China?**
Personal cryptocurrency trading is not illegal in China. The 2021 regulations target mining operations and domestic exchange businesses, not individual traders using overseas platforms.

**Which exchange is better for beginners?**
OKX has better Chinese language support and copy trading features. Binance has better liquidity and more coin options. Recommendation: register both.

**How to get fee discounts?**
1. Register through a referral link (permanent 10-20% discount)
2. Hold BNB (Binance) or OKB (OKX) for token-based fee discounts
3. Use limit orders (Maker fee < Taker fee)
4. Increase VIP level through trading volume
